Frequently asked questions

When does billing start for Subscribe?
Your first 3 months are completely free. On the first day of month 4, your card will be automatically charged $5/month. You can cancel before then with no charge.
What counts as "security advisory" in the Consultancy plan?
Written recommendations on security policies, risk assessments, SOPs, vendor selection, and compliance questions. This is advisory only — not a substitute for licensed legal, law enforcement, or operational security services.
What happens to my 2 free topics if I upgrade?
All 6 topic categories are unlocked immediately. Your existing selections are preserved and you can add or change topics at any time from your profile settings.
How is the Monday briefing delivered?
Every Monday morning, a curated digest of the week's most important security updates is sent to your registered email address. It covers your selected topics and includes regulatory alerts and key developments from across Asia.
Can I submit articles as a subscriber?
Yes. Subscribe-tier users can submit Professional Insights — analysis, case studies, or commentary from their own experience. Submissions are reviewed before publishing. AI-generated content fills the section when no submissions are pending.
Is the Consultancy plan suitable for a full company?
The Consultancy plan is designed for one organization's security team or one point-of-contact per company. Multi-seat enterprise plans are available — contact us to discuss your needs.
