Perkapolri No. 4/2026 New
Peraturan Kapolri — Badan Usaha Jasa Pengamanan (BUJP)
Updated licensing, reporting, and operational requirements for all private security service companies operating in Indonesia. Introduces mandatory digital incident reporting and minimum staffing ratio requirements per site classification.
Published: 1 Apr 2026
BNSP Rev.3 — 2026 Updated
Standar Kompetensi Kerja Nasional — Satpam & Corporate Security
Revised national competency framework for security personnel. Key additions: emergency first-aid module, crisis communication, and mandatory annual refresher training. Effective July 2026 for all new contracts.
Effective: Jul 2026
UU No. 2/2002 Active
Undang-Undang Kepolisian Negara Republik Indonesia
Foundation law governing the Indonesian National Police and its relationship with private security. Defines the legal boundary between state security functions and private security operations. Still the primary reference for BUJP compliance.
Last reviewed: 2024
Permen Kemenaker No. 5/2023 Active
Peraturan Menteri Ketenagakerjaan — K3 Satuan Pengamanan
Occupational health and safety requirements specific to security personnel. Covers equipment standards, working hour limits, and mandatory health screening for armed security roles. Relevant for all HR and procurement teams engaging security vendors.
Active since: Mar 2023
SNI 8900:2020 Active
Standar Nasional Indonesia — Sistem Manajemen Keamanan
National standard for security management systems aligned with ISO 31000. Provides a framework for risk-based security planning applicable to corporate, industrial, and government facilities across Indonesia.
Published: 2020
Perkapolri No. 24/2007 Active
Sistem Manajemen Pengamanan Organisasi, Perusahaan, dan/atau Instansi
Establishes the Security Management System (SMS) framework for organizations, companies, and government institutions in Indonesia. Foundation reference for corporate security planning and audit readiness.
Active since: 2007
🌐 International Standards 4 standards
ASIS PSP — 2024 Ed. Active
ASIS Physical Security Professional Standard
The global benchmark for physical security management. Covers threat assessment, countermeasure selection, and security program design. The PSP certification is widely recognized by multinational corporations operating in Asia.
Edition: 2024 · ASIS International
ISO 31000:2018 Draft 2026
Risk Management — Guidelines
International standard providing principles and guidelines for risk management applicable across any sector. ISO 31000:2026 draft is currently under review, with significant updates to digital risk integration and climate-related security risks expected.
Draft under review · ISO/TC 262
ISO 27001:2022 Active
Information Security Management Systems
Relevant to security professionals overseeing access control systems, CCTV networks, and integrated security platforms. Defines requirements for establishing and maintaining an information security management system (ISMS).
Current edition: 2022
BS 7858:2019 Active
Screening of Individuals Working in a Secure Environment
British Standard widely adopted by multinational security programs in Asia for background screening of security personnel. Covers identity verification, employment history, criminal record checks, and reference standards for high-security environments.
